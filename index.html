Speech Therapy in Nepal

Diverse Way Clinic is a trusted speech therapy centre in Kathmandu, serving families across Nepal with certified speech-language pathologists.

Whether your child has a speech delay, difficulty with sounds, stuttering, or a language disorder, our speech therapists create personalised plans that build confidence at home, school, and in daily life. We also support adults with voice, fluency, and communication needs.

Located in Shankhamul, Lalitpur — easily reachable from Kathmandu — we offer assessment, one-to-one sessions, and parent guidance. Book speech therapy in Nepal by phone (9845366417), WhatsApp, or our online booking form.
